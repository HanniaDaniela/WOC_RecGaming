About



RecGaming was formed in a University of Central Florida classroom with six young ambitious students.
Certainly not for a class grade. RecGaming is set out to bring enjoyment to all kinds of people.
Whether it's using a dark narrative game or purely using humor through social media.



RecGaming = Recreational Gaming.




We figured recreational would be the best choice for gaming. Usually recreational refers to leisure time,
meaning you are only here to relax and enjoy some gameplay.
